Open Air: Sarah McLeod

From sweeping hilltops to hidden lakes and spectacular ocean-scapes, Open Air is a beautiful collision of music, people and place. Featuring some of Australia’s most loved musicians in intimate outdoor concerts set against the Sunshine Coast’s spectacular natural environment, the Open Air experience is not to be missed.  

Perched overlooking the sea, rock-goddess Sarah McLeod will captivate audiences with an exclusive stripped back performance at Dicky Beach.  

The creative force behind ARIA Award-winning, platinum-selling rockers The Superjesus, Sarah McLeod brings her magnetic stage-presence and rock pedigree to this intimate Open Air concert experience, accompanied by a baby-grand piano and the crashing waves of the ocean. 

Rug up and settle in to be lulled by the serene sounds of One Electric Lady, Sarah McLeod, as the sun sets.  

Seating will be provided at this event. Please wear warm clothing as it gets cold once the sun sets.
