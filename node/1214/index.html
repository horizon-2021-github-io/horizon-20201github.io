Kabi Kabi Connections

Kabi Kabi Connections is a dynamic and inspiring large-scale projection of artworks by esteemed Kabi Kabi visual artists. 

Co-curated by Kabi Kabi Traditional Custodian and arts and cultural advocate, Aunty Helena Gulash, this new media work creates visibility for the stories of Kabi Kabi people and contemporary representations of Country. 

The artworks will be projected at various sites across Kabi Kabi Country. 

Locations

  • USC Art Gallery exterior wall (via projection)
  • Sunshine Plaza
